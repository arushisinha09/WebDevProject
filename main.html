The Legacy...

Sports has grown tremendously over the past few years and is gaining popularity all over the world. Badminton is no less and has made its distinctive place. The legacy goes long back when Pro Corporate League came into existence, a league which was started to reunite corporate cricket lovers and to keep the passion alive. Its decade long experience and association of 200+ renowned corporates, major celebrities, and Media houses makes it grand and unique. Its 16 acres lush green PCL MULTI SPORTS ARENA lies in the heart of Noida expressway with 3 grounds equipped with live telecast and commentary fixtures. Slowly but effectively, Pro corporate league ventured into different sports and today has made badminton reach the right people who are passionate about the game.
